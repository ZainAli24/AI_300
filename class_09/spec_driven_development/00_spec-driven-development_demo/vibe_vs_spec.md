
# **1. Refined version — tumhare alfaaz mein**

## **Vibe coding**
Vibe coding mein hum apne mood ya feeling ke mutabiq bina pehle planning ke seedha kuch bana dete hain. Jo cheez vibe se banai jati hai wo aam tor pe scalable ya long-term ke liye nahi hoti, isliye usko maintain karna mushkil hota hai.

## **Spec-driven development**
Spec-driven development mein pehle product ka poora plan (PRD) ya requirements likhte hain. Phir us plan ko chote-chote steps mein tod kar, ek ek step final karte hue development karte hain. Har step clear hone par agla step shuru hota hai — is tarah product scalable aur long-term use ke laayak banta hai.

**Short difference (ek line)**

- Vibe = jaldi aur feeling se (fast but hard to maintain).  

- Spec-driven = pehle plan, phir step-by-step (slower but reliable aur scalable).
